## Calibration / Verification Procedure (Do This Once)

> **No nonsense approach:**  
The DS18B20 is factory calibrated. You do **verification**, then apply a **single offset correction** if needed.

---

### Step 1: Ice Water Test (0 °C Reference)

1. Fill a glass with **crushed ice**
2. Add a small amount of water to form a **slush**  
   (ice should not be freely floating)
3. Insert the **DS18B20 sensor**  
   - Do **not** touch the glass sides or bottom
4. Wait approximately **2 minutes** for stabilization

**Expected reading:**
```text
0.0 °C ± 0.5 °C
